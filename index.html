Facts Department TTK

The Faculty of Mathematics and Statistics was established in 1976, along with the formation of Ho Chi Minh City University of Economics. For nearly 50 years, the Faculty has trained thousands of students at all levels and training systems from Bachelor, Master to Doctorate for the country.

The Faculty of Mathematics and Statistics currently has 04 subjects: Basic Mathematics, Economic Mathematics, Statistics - Data Analysis and Financial Mathematics, with a teaching staff of 30 people. The Faculty offers undergraduate training with two majors: Economic Mathematics and Economic Statistics. The field of Economic Mathematics has two majors: Risk Analysis and Actuarial Science (Actuarial Science) and Financial Mathematics (Financial Mathematics). The Economic Statistics major has a specialization in Business Statistics. The Risk Analysis and Actuarial major has a partial English program. The Faculty also offers master's degree training in Economic Statistics; Master's degree in Economic Mathematics; and PhD in Statistics.
